Sandvich Edible Device

Description

The Sandvich is a great tool for a Heavy defending an area, as it gives him the ability to replenish health between enemy waves without having to abandon his post.

Ingredients

  • 1 thick slice of bread
  • 1 fresh Iceberg lettuce leaf
  • A few slices of a tomato
  • A slice of Swiss cheese
  • A few slices of ham and bologna
  • OPTIONAL: 1 pimento stuffed and a toothpick
  1. Take the slice of bread and cut it in half (however you want).
  2. Put the slices of meat on the base bread.
  3. Place the cheese slices on top.
  4. Place the tomato slices on top.
  5. Place the lettuce leaf on top.
  6. Place the top bread to complete the sandvich.
  7. OPTIONAL: Pierce the stuffed olive with a toothpick.
  8. Pierce toothpick through the middle of the Sandvich.

Enjoy Sandvich Comrade! Om nom nom, nom nom...
